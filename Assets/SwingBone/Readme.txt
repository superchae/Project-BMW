Swing Bone applies a simple physical effect on character's bones or joints, 
and takes the natural shaking / fluttering effect of hair, clothes, tail, breasts or any part in just one step.



------------------------------------------------------
INSTALLATION:
------------------------------------------------------
To install Swing Bone simply import all files from the unitypackage.


------------------------------------------------------
QUICKSTART:
------------------------------------------------------
Once you have installed Swing Bone you should be able to see the Swing Bone in your Component menu [Component]->[Physics]->[Swing Bone].
To start swing your bones, select parent bone, click on Component > Physics -> Swing Bone
This will attach a new Swing Bone component in your bone.
